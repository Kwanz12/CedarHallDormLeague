Dorm League Rule-Book

Rules

DISCLAIMERS!! Your aim is always to be better than the previous captain because the aim of dorm league is to build leaders. Therefore you can hold training sessions, squad meetings, anything.
Transfer season is in October and ends in November both captains have to agree that the player is not a slave and therefore has a say in the matter.
To decide the man of the match the sports director and the two captains that played the match and the sports director will decide the man of the match and if a decision is not made between the three of us we will invite the referee to make a decision but he will have to choose from the names we have selected.
If there is a request for a time change, both captains will have to agree on that time change, if no agreement is reached then the match will be played at the original time that was set.
Depending on how things go, Some matches *MIGHT be scheduled as Saturday Night Matches.
Linesmen will have a specific color, that color has to be the opposite of the two teams that are playing.
At the end of the cup a Ballon d'Or will be awarded to the best player of the season. There will also be awards for Captain of the season. (This involves holding training, and squad meetings etc), Goalkeeper of the Season, Defender of the season and Top scorer and Assister of the season.
1. 8 players will be the maximum amount of players on the field at any point in time , players can be substituted back on to the field after being substituted off the field (a minimum of 5 players are required to play the match)
2. No Off Dorm people are allowed to play (If you want to play you will have to speak to the dean about that.)
3. Red, Black, Green and Blue only (if you don't have a red or blue shirt representing your team, you will NOT be able to play)
4. Corner rules apply 5. Indecent language on the field can result in a red card (taken off the field and 1 match ban)
6. Prayer will occur before every match
7. Hand balls or being fouled in front of the goal (if you can stretch both hands out and touch the incident) will result in a penalty
8. Penalty kicks will be half of the field and an empty goal
9. After a foul players (who committed the foul) have to take 5 steps away from the ball
10. The goal keeper will have the final say if the ball is out of bounds towards the goal line (this rule only applies if there is no referee/Linesman)
11. Players who get substituted will have to wait until every player on the bench is given a chance on the field (after every player is given a chance on the field , the order in which players go on doesn't matter)
12. During a freekick only one player is allowed to be inside the goal.
13. Referees can call for a VAR check at any point in time as long as there are cameras.
14. Expletives can result in a red card.
15. If a captain wants to transfer a player to their side the starter will be $500 and depending on the quality of the player or if the captain (who will accept the transfer) doesn't want to let them go for that amount the money can increase, the money will go to the XLCR club.
